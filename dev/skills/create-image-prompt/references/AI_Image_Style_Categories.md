## A. Photorealism / Real-World Styles

- **Photorealistic** — Like a real photograph
- **Ultra-realistic** — High detail, sharp everywhere
- **RAW realism** — Raw file tone, unedited colors

- **Street photography** — Daily life documentary style
- **Documentary** — Real storytelling, natural feeling
- **Journalism style** — News photography style
- **Candid realism** — Natural candid shots
- **Film photography** (35mm, 50mm, 120mm) — Authentic film look

### 2) Commercial Realism

- **Lifestyle photography** — Brand-style daily life
- **Beauty commercial** — Focus on skin and beauty

## M. Experimental / Hybrid

- **Mixed-media** — Mix of painting and photography techniques
- **Collage** — Cut and paste multiple images together
- **Double exposure** — Two scenes overlaid as one image
- **Photo-bashing** — Multiple photo sources combined into new scene
- **Texture overload** — Emphasis on multiple dense textures
- **Deconstruction style** — Broken apart and rearranged experimentally
